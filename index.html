About Me

Yunjeong Lee is a PhD student in School of Mathematics and Computing (Computational Science and Engineering) at Yonsei University in South Korea. Her advisor is Jeehyun Lee, who is a professor in School of Mathematics and Computing (Mathematics) at Yonsei University.

She is interested in modeling of epidemic diseases using deterministic ordinary differential equations. She usually solves inverse problems for model calibration and assesses control strategies. She is currently working on modeling HIV, A(H1N1)pdm09, and SARS-CoV-2.

At Spain Square in Sevilla, Spain
